Our Story

Where Marrakech
meets Ethiopia

Nestled in the prestigious Hivernage district, opposite the Royal Tennis Club, Marh Coffee Roasters is dedicated to a single origin — the highlands of Ethiopia. We source exclusively 100% Arabica beans, roasted in small batches to preserve their complex, balanced flavour.

Awarded TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice 2024 and ranked #15 of 84 cafés in Marrakech, Marh is a destination for discerning coffee lovers, travellers, and locals seeking an exceptional experience in the heart of Morocco.
